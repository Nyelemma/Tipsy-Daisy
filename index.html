A little sip of our story

We rolled onto the scene in a horsebox with big dreams and even bigger ice buckets.

Tipsy Daisy began life trotting around fields; now she’s dressed up for your dance floor. Our converted horsebox is a hand-crafted bar on wheels—warm wood, soft lighting, and a wink of whimsy in every detail.

We love being part of the moments that matter: the “I do”, the end-of-year toast, the garden party that runs past sunset. It’s not just about what’s in the glass—it’s the smile when someone hands you the perfect G&T.
